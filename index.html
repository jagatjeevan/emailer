Hi, ThoughtWorkers, The time has arrived to acknowledge the contribution made by each and every one of you in making the ThoughtWorks journey a resounding success. It is imperative now to visit individual performances and experiences over the last 6 months, reflect on how each of us performed as an individual contributor, and understand the relative contribution as a member of the overall organization.

Sharing the feedback is a continuous activity and we hope that you are collecting feedback on a regular basis which you can start collating. If you still have not, please start exchanging the feedback with people you have worked with, add your self-reflections, create a summary, and set up a review meeting with the relevant stakeholders.

Take time in collating all the achievements done in the past 6 months. This could be anything you have done which impacted the clients and they have acknowledged, anything which impacted the team in quicker delivery of the stories, etc.

Seek Feedback

Collect feedback on performance and personal Development from colleagues, account leads, Ops team, CDP Mentor, Community reps.

Schedule

Block the calendar of the stakeholders

Timelines

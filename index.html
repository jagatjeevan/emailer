It is time for Annual Performance & Development Review 2020

Time has come to acknowledge the contribution made by each one of you in making ThoughtWorks journey a success. It is time to visit individual performance and experiences over the last 6 months, reflect on how we performed as an individual contributor, and understand the relative contribution as a member of the organization.

Sharing feedback is a continuous activity and we hope you have done that already. If you still have not, please start exchanging feedback with people you have worked with, add your self-reflections, create a summary and set up a Review Meeting with relevant stakeholders.

Let us begin by having a look at critical aspects of the entire process:
Schedule

the discussion ahead of time

Prepare

the discussion points covering outcomes, key skills & behaviour

Evaluate Self

against the set goals and share it with your project lead.

Seek feedback

on performance & personal Development

Commit

to be open minded and communicate clearly

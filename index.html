It is time for
Annual Performance Development
Review - 2020

Hi, ThoughtWorkers, Time has come to acknowledge the contribution made by each one of you in making ThoughtWorks journey a success. It is time to visit individual performance and experiences over the last 6 months, reflect on how we performed as an individual contributor, and understand the relative contribution as a member of the organization.

Sharing feedback is a continuous activity and we hope you have done that already. If you still have not, please start exchanging feedback with people you have worked with, add your self-reflections, create a summary and set up a Review Meeting with relevant stakeholders.

Let us look at critical aspects of the entire process:

Here are some preparatory tips to follow during the review

Schedule

Schedule the discussion ahead of time

Seggregate

Seggregate all the points covering the outcomes, key skills and behaviour in your feedback.

Evaluate Self

Evaluate self against the set goals and share it with your project lead.

Seek feedback

on performance & personal Development

Commit

to be open minded and communicate clearly
